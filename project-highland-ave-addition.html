Home Addition — Highland Ave, MA

A full home addition on Highland Ave — new living space built onto the existing structure, fully framed, insulated, finished, and permitted from the ground up.

Highland Ave, MA Home Addition Fully Permitted Completed

About This Project

This homeowner on Highland Ave needed more living space without the disruption and cost of moving. We built a full addition onto the existing structure — expanding the footprint of the home with new square footage that flows naturally from the original layout. The addition was fully permitted and passed all required inspections.

The project covered everything from foundation work and framing through insulation, drywall, electrical, and final finishes. The result is a seamless extension of the home — matching the existing roofline, siding, and interior finishes so the addition looks like it was always there.

Work Completed
  • Foundation and structural work for new addition footprint
  • Full framing — walls, floor system, roof tie-in
  • Insulation — walls, ceiling, and floor to current code
  • Drywall, taping, and finish work throughout
  • Electrical rough-in and finish — outlets, lighting, panel tie-in
  • Exterior siding and roofing matched to existing home
  • Windows and exterior doors installed
  • Building and electrical permits — all inspections passed
