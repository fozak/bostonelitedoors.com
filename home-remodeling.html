Boston Homes Are Worth The Investment

Greater Boston's housing market consistently ranks among the strongest in the country. Remodeling here isn't just about comfort — it directly impacts appraisal value, sale price, and how fast your home moves.

Why Greater Boston homeowners remodel now
  • Pre-1980 kitchens and baths significantly hurt resale value in this market
  • Deferred maintenance compounds — a dated bathroom becomes a structural issue
  • Permit-required work done without permits causes title problems at closing
  • Spring and summer contractor slots book out 6–10 weeks in advance
What remodeling returns in Greater Boston
  • Minor kitchen remodel returns 80–85% of cost at sale
  • Bathroom renovation adds $10,000–$25,000 to appraised value
  • Finished basement adds 10–20% to usable square footage
  • Financing available — spread the cost over time at competitive rates
