Paved Driveway — Stoughton, MA

Full driveway removal and asphalt repaving in Stoughton — old surface demolished, base regraded for proper drainage, and finished with fresh asphalt and clean defined edges.

Stoughton, MA Asphalt Driveway Completed

About This Project

This Stoughton homeowner had an aging driveway that had deteriorated badly — cracked surface, uneven grade, and poor drainage causing water to pool near the foundation. We handled the full replacement from demolition through finished paving: old material broken up and hauled away, base layer regraded and compacted, and fresh asphalt laid with clean defined edges throughout.

The finished driveway slopes correctly away from the house for proper water runoff, and the clean asphalt edges give the front of the property a sharp, well-maintained look. The whole job was completed in a single day.

Work Completed
  • Full demolition and removal of existing driveway surface
  • Base layer excavation, grading, and compaction
  • Drainage slope corrected — water directed away from foundation
  • Fresh asphalt installation — hot mix, properly compacted
  • Clean defined edges throughout
  • All demolition debris hauled off-site
  • Completed in a single day — minimal disruption
