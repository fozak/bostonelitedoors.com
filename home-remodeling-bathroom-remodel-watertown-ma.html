About This Project

This Watertown homeowner (02472) discovered their shower walls had failed waterproofing around a window opening — a common issue in older Watertown homes where original construction left gaps between window frames and the shower surround. Water was getting behind the walls, creating a moisture problem that needed to be addressed before it caused structural damage. Boston Door Company responded quickly, assessed the scope, and worked with the homeowner on pricing when they offered to source some of the materials themselves.

The team stripped out the damaged wall material, resealed around the window frame, and installed new tile throughout the shower surround. After completion, the homeowner flagged a minor concern and the crew returned promptly to address it — no argument, no delay. That kind of follow-through is standard practice at Boston Door Company and reflects the accountability that keeps clients coming back.
