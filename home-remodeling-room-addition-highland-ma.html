About This Project

This Highland homeowner (01529) needed more living space but didn't want to move. A full room addition was the right solution — expanding the home's footprint while seamlessly integrating with the existing structure. Boston Door Company managed the project from permit filing through final inspection, handling every trade in-house: foundation prep, framing, roofing tie-in, insulation, electrical rough-in, drywall, and finish work. The result is a fully conditioned, permitted addition that looks and functions like it was always part of the house.

Room additions in the Highland and Sutton area often require careful attention to local zoning setbacks and building code requirements — particularly for structures that tie into existing roof pitches or foundation walls. Boston Door Company handled all permitting and inspections, ensuring the finished addition met every code requirement without delays. The project was completed on schedule and within the agreed budget.
