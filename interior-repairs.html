Massachusetts Homes Have Specific Needs

Greater Boston's older housing stock, freeze-thaw cycles, and humid summers create interior wear patterns that general handymen often underestimate. Elite Contractors knows what to look for.

Common issues in Greater Boston homes
  • Plaster cracking from seasonal expansion and contraction
  • Water stains and drywall damage from ice dam leaks
  • Drafty doors and windows in pre-1980s construction
  • Hardwood cupping and warping from basement humidity
  • Paint peeling on north-facing walls and in bathrooms
Why professional finishing pays off
  • Proper repairs prevent moisture from spreading behind walls
  • Energy-efficient windows reduce heating costs by up to 25%
  • Quality interior finishes add real value at appraisal time
  • MA building code compliance handled — permits included when required
