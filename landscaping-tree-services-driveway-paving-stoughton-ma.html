About This Project

This Stoughton homeowner (02072) needed a full driveway replacement — the existing surface had deteriorated through years of freeze-thaw cycles typical of southeastern Massachusetts winters. Boston Door Company handled the entire project: excavation of the old surface, base grading for proper drainage, compacted gravel sub-base, and fresh asphalt installation with clean-cut edges. The result is a driveway that drains correctly, handles heavy vehicles, and will hold up through the region's demanding seasonal conditions.

Stoughton driveways face particular challenges from the town's clay-heavy soil, which shifts with frost and can undermine a poorly prepared base. Boston Door Company' approach — proper excavation depth, compacted gravel base, and quality asphalt — addresses those conditions directly. The crew worked efficiently, completed the job in a single day, and left the site clean. Boston Door Company serves the full South Shore corridor including Stoughton, Canton, Brockton, Randolph, and Sharon.
