Boston Properties Need Attention

New England's storms, wet winters, and dense tree cover create real risks for Greater Boston homeowners. Don't wait for a tree to become an emergency.

Warning signs on your property
  • Dead or leaning trees near the house
  • Branches touching or overhanging the roof
  • Visible root damage or soil heaving
  • Standing water or poor drainage after rain
  • Overgrown shrubs blocking windows or walkways
Benefits of acting now
  • Emergency tree removal costs 3–5× more than planned removal
  • Fallen trees can cause $20,000+ in property damage
  • Spring is the busiest season — book early for best scheduling
  • Healthy landscaping adds 10–15% to home resale value
