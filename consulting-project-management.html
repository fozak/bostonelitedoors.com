Why Boston Homeowners Need This

Greater Boston has one of the most complex home improvement environments in the country — strict permitting, dense housing, aging stock, and contractors who are often overbooked and under-communicative. Most homeowners go in blind and pay for it.

Common problems we prevent
  • Contractors who start work without permits — creating title problems at closing
  • Quotes that leave out demolition, disposal, or finish work — then surprise you mid-project
  • Projects that stall for weeks with no communication and no clear timeline
  • Change orders that inflate the original price by 20–40%
  • Work that fails inspection because a licensed sub wasn't used
  • HOA or historic district violations discovered after work is complete
What proper project management delivers
  • Clear written scope before any money changes hands
  • All permits pulled and inspections scheduled in advance
  • Weekly progress updates — you always know where things stand
  • Issues caught and resolved before they affect your schedule or budget
  • Final project that passes inspection and adds real value to your home
