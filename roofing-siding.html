Boston Winters Don't Wait

Small issues become expensive problems once temperatures drop. Acting now saves thousands.

Signs you need attention now
  • Missing or curling shingles
  • Water stains on ceilings or walls
  • Ice dams forming at roof edge
  • Granules accumulating in gutters
  • Visible sagging or soft spots
Why act before winter
  • Ice dams cause $4,000–$15,000 in interior damage
  • Frozen ground delays repairs until spring
  • Emergency rates are 2–3× standard pricing
  • Most insurers require prompt damage reporting
